? How can I buy?

After our product reveal, a mint date will be announced. Once you mint the NFT you can trade on all major marketplaces or redeem for your physical figure.

? What does the NFT get me?

Your NFT can be redeemed for a limited edition figure. Your NFT will also get you access into the Sleek Geek community and future Sleek Geek benefits/suprises.

? How can I burn and redeem my NFT?

The first step is minting your NFT. Shortly after the mint, you will then be able to redeem your figure by burning the NFT and providing your shipping details.

? How big are these figures going to be?

Figures will stand 4-6 inches tall.

? When does the production start?

After the mint is complete, production will begin.
